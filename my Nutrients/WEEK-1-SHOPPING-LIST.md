## Shopping order (efficient trip)

1. **Proteins first** — chicken, eggs, curd (refrigerated)
2. **Staples** — rice, atta, dal
3. **Vegetables & fruit** — last before checkout
4. **Supplements** — pharmacy / online
5. **Home** — portion chicken into ~350 g bags, freeze 3–4 days worth

---

## Week 1 — what NOT to overbuy

| Skip | Why |
|------|-----|
| Red meat × 7 days | Max 1–2× **month** — optional 200 g only |
| Protein bars / junk "bulk" snacks | Whole food + whey is enough |
| Multivitamin | Overlaps D3/B12 — avoid |
| Second protein powder | One whey tub is fine |

# Sample Day — 4 Meals (Indian, Lean Bulk)

> **Training day target:** ~3,050 kcal | 170 g protein | ~420 g carbs | ~68 g fat  
> Weigh chicken, rice, roti, whey — estimate vegetables/oil.

---

## Meal 1 — Breakfast (~650 kcal | 38 P | 75 C | 22 F)

- 3 whole eggs + 1 egg white (scrambled/boiled)
- 2 medium roti **or** 80 g dry oats
- 1 banana
- Black coffee or tea (optional)

---

## Meal 2 — Lunch (~850 kcal | 52 P | 110 C | 18 F)

- **200 g cooked chicken breast** (weigh raw ~170 g)
- 1.5 cups cooked rice (~200 g cooked)
- 1 cup dal
- Mixed vegetables + salad
- 1 tsp ghee or cooking oil

---

## Meal 3 — Pre/Post Workout Snack (~450 kcal | 42 P | 55 C | 5 F)

- **1 scoop whey** (~25 g protein) in water/milk
- 1 banana or 2 dates
- **Optional on boxing days:** extra 50 g rice or 1 roti

---

## Meal 4 — Dinner (~750 kcal | 38 P | 90 C | 22 F)

- **150 g chicken** or 4 egg whites + 2 whole eggs
- 2 roti **or** 1 cup rice
- Curd (200 g) — protein + probiotics
- Sabzi (vegetables)

---

## Red Meat — 1–2× Per Month (Optional)

Use strategically; **keep B12 500 mcg daily** regardless.

| Option | Portion | Protein | Best timing |
|--------|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Mutton / lean beef** | 150–200 g cooked | ~35–45 g | **Weight training day** lunch or dinner |
| **Liver** (if you eat it) | 50–75 g | ~12–18 g | Once — very high B12; don't mega-dose |

**Why include it:** Natural B12, iron, zinc, creatine — supports your deficiencies without extra pills.

**Swap:** Replace chicken in **Meal 2 or 4** with red meat — keep rice/roti/dal the same. No need to exceed 170 g protein that day.

**Don't:** Skip methylcobalamin on red-meat days — one meal won't fix 237 pg/mL B12 or replace daily dosing.

---

Drop **~300 kcal** from carbs (remove snack roti/banana or reduce rice by ½ cup).

- **3–4 L water/day** minimum
- **+500 ml** on boxing days
- Pinch of salt pre-long session if sweating heavily

# Flexible Training-Day Nutrition

> Use this when your weekly schedule isn't fixed.  
> **Tag each day** in your app: `REST` · `WEIGHT` · `BOXING`

---

## Your 3 Day Types

| Tag | When | Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|-----|------|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| **REST** | No training | **2,750** | 170 g | 320 g | 72 g |
| **WEIGHT** | Upper / lower / mix | **3,000** | 170 g | 400 g | 70 g |
| **BOXING** | Learning / cardio boxing | **2,900** | 170 g | 380 g | 68 g |

**Protein is always 170 g.** Only carbs and total calories move.

---

## How to Adjust Carbs (Simple Rule)

On any **training day**, add **~80 g carbs** vs rest (~320 g → ~400 g):

- +1 cup cooked rice, **or**
- +2 rotis, **or**
- +2 bananas, **or**
- +1 roti + 1 banana

**Boxing days:** use the middle row (~380 g carbs) — high cardio but lower muscular load than heavy leg day.

**Weight days (especially legs):** use the high row (~400 g carbs).

## Weekly Pattern (Example — Swap Days Freely)

| Mon | Tue | Wed | Thu | Fri | Sat | Sun |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| WEIGHT | BOXING | WEIGHT | REST | BOXING | WEIGHT | REST |

- **5 training days** ✓  
- **2 rest days** ✓  
- **D3 60k IU** — pick one day (e.g. Sunday) regardless of rest/train

---

## Weekly Calorie Average

With 3 weight + 2 boxing + 2 rest:

`(3×3000 + 2×2900 + 2×2750) ÷ 7 = **2,929 kcal/day**`

→ ~**0.3 kg/week** scale gain — appropriate for **lean bulk**.

---

## Scale Check (Every Sunday Morning)

| Weekly gain | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| **<0.2 kg** | Add **+150 kcal/day** (extra roti or 30 g rice) |
| **0.25–0.4 kg** | Perfect — hold targets |
| **>0.5 kg** | Drop **−150 kcal/day** on rest days first |

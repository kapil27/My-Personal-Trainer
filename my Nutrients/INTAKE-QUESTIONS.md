# Intake Questionnaire — Round 1

Answer what you can. Skip nothing if possible — each answer sharpens your plan.

---

## 1. About You

1. **Age?**
2. **Sex / biological sex?** (affects BMR, iron needs, hormone context)
3. **Country / city?** (food availability, sun exposure for D3)
4. **Any medical conditions?** (thyroid, diabetes, kidney, liver, gut issues)
5. **Any food allergies or intolerances?** (lactose, gluten, nuts, etc.)
6. **Are you vegetarian except chicken/eggs, or do you eat other meats/fish too?**

---

## 2. Training (Critical for macros)

7. **Training style?** (PPL, bro split, full body, CrossFit, etc.)
8. **Days per week training?**
9. **Session length?**
10. **Cardio?** How much and what type?
11. **Current strength benchmarks?** (optional — helps gauge training age)
12. **Sleep hours per night on average?**

---

## 3. Current Diet

13. **Meals per day?** (2, 3, 4, 5+)
14. **Do you cook at home or eat out often?**
15. **Current daily protein intake estimate?** (rough guess in grams)
16. **Current calorie intake estimate?** (or "no idea")
17. **Dairy?** Milk, curd, paneer, whey — yes/no/how much?
18. **Rice vs roti vs both?** Primary carb source?
19. **Vegetables & fruits daily?** Rough servings?
20. **Alcohol?** Frequency?
21. **Caffeine?** Coffee/tea — how many cups?

---

## 4. Supplements — Details Needed

22. **D3 60,000 IU — how often?** (once/week? daily? prescribed by doctor?)
23. **B12 tablet — brand/dose?** (e.g. 1500 mcg methylcobalamin daily?)
24. **Creatine — have you used before?** Monohydrate? Planned dose?
25. **Calcium — why taking it?** Dose? (Watch: don't overload with D3 without K2)
26. **L-Carnitine — oral or injectable?** Dose? (Oral has weak evidence for muscle gain)
27. **Magnesium — which form?** (glycinate, citrate, oxide?)
28. **Fish oil — EPA/DHA mg per capsule?**

---

## 5. Goals & Preferences

29. **Target weight in 3 months?** 77 kg scale weight, or "look bigger at same weight"?
30. **Priority:** Max muscle vs stay lean vs don't care about slight fat gain?
31. **Budget for food/supplements?** (monthly rough range)
32. **Foods you love and won't give up?**
33. **Foods you hate or won't eat?**
34. **Meal prep time available?** (15 min, 30 min, 1 hr per meal?)
35. **Will you weigh food on a scale?** (essential for "rogue mode")

---

## 6. Blood Work & Health

36. **Latest blood test values?** (D3 ng/mL, B12 pg/mL, ferritin, testosterone, lipid panel if available)
37. **Iron levels?** (common with B12/D deficiency patterns)
38. **Doctor's advice on the 3-month bulk?**

---

## 7. App Preferences (for later build)

39. **Track macros only, or also micros** (vitamins, minerals)?
40. **Barcode scanning for packaged foods?**
41. **Indian food database priority?** (dal, roti, biryani portions)
42. **Offline mode needed?**
43. **Reminders for meals/supplements/water?**

# Supplement Protocol — FINAL (Blood-Work Adjusted)

> **Based on:** D3 = 8.77 ng/mL (severe), B12 = 237 pg/mL (low), Hb = 15.6 (normal)  
> Confirm with your prescribing doctor. Retest at **Week 12**.

---

## Daily (Every Day)

| # | Supplement | Dose | When | Notes |
|---|------------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | **Creatine monohydrate** | 5 g | Any time | With water; no cycling needed |
| 2 | **B12 (methylcobalamin)** | **500 mcg** | Morning, empty stomach | Critical — red meat only 1–2×/month |
| 3 | **Fish oil** | **1–2 softgels/day** | Lunch or dinner | **EPA 1000 mg/softgel** — check DHA on label (see below) |
| 4 | **Magnesium glycinate** | **300 mg** elemental | **30–60 min before bed** | Sleep + D3 activation + recovery |
| 5 | **Vitamin K2 (MK-7)** | **100 mcg** | Daily | Essential with high-dose D3 protocol |
| 6 | **Whey protein** | 1 scoop (~25 g P) | Post-workout or snack | To reach 170 g/day total protein |

---

## Weekly (Same Day Each Week)

| Supplement | Dose | When |
|------------|------|------|
| **Vitamin D3** | **60,000 IU** | **Once/week** with fatty meal (eggs + ghee or with fish oil meal) |

**Pick a fixed day** (e.g., Sunday breakfast) — log it in your app.

---

## Fish Oil — Your Product (EPA 1000 mg)

| Label reads | Daily dose |
|-------------|------------|
| EPA 1000 mg + DHA **200–500 mg** per softgel | **2 softgels/day** → ~2.4–3 g EPA+DHA ✓ |
| EPA 1000 mg + DHA **under 200 mg** | **2 softgels/day** + consider higher-DHA brand later |
| "1000 mg fish oil" (not EPA alone) | Read fine print — may need **3–4 caps** |

Take with food to avoid fishy burps. Split **1 cap lunch + 1 cap dinner** if GI sensitive.

## Skip / Deprioritize

| Supplement | Verdict |
|------------|---------|
| **Calcium** | **Skip for now** — dairy in diet + severe D3 correction; add K2 instead of extra calcium |
| **L-Carnitine** | **Optional** — 2 g pre-workout if you want; not required for muscle gain |
| **Iron** | **Do not take** — Hb 15.6 is normal; unsafe without ferritin confirmation |
| **Zinc** | **Optional 15 mg** with dinner if no multivitamin — only if you feel run-down |

## Week 12 Decision Tree

| Retest Result | Action |
|---------------|--------|
| D3 **≥40 ng/mL** | Doctor may switch to **maintenance** (e.g., 60k monthly or 1000–2000 IU daily) |
| D3 **20–40 ng/mL** | Continue weekly 60k for another 4–8 weeks |
| B12 **≥400 pg/mL** | Continue 500 mcg or reduce per doctor |
| B12 **<400 pg/mL** | Increase to **1000 mcg/day** or discuss injection |
